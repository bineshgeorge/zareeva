A Digital Twin for Every Product

Zareeva connects each product to a secure digital identity that acts as its digital twin.

This identity records lifecycle events and enables both authentication and supply chain traceability.
Together, these capabilities allow products to be authenticated, tracked, and verified across their lifecycle.

    A secure digital identity for every product

    Zareeva assigns a secure digital identity to every product unit. This identity acts as a digital twin that anchors authentication, lifecycle tracking, and traceability throughout the product journey. By establishing a persistent identity layer, brands gain a trusted reference point for every interaction with the product.

    Generate secure identities for every product unit
    Support multiple code formats including QR and serialized identifiers
    Associate identities with product metadata and manufacturing information
    Maintain a persistent identity layer across the product lifecycle

    Capture and record product activity across its lifecycle.

    Zareeva records events as products move through manufacturing, packaging, distribution, retail, and customer interaction points. Each event becomes part of a verifiable lifecycle history associated with the product’s digital identity.

    Capture lifecycle events across supply chain nodes
    Record scans, movements, and verification interactions
    Maintain a chronological event history for every product
    Enable real-time visibility into product activity

    Verify product authenticity with a simple scan.

    Authentication allows partners, distributors, and consumers to verify product authenticity using the product’s digital identity. Each scan confirms whether a product is genuine and provides contextual information about its lifecycle.

    Verify product authenticity through secure scans
    Detect duplicate, suspicious, or invalid product codes
    Enable consumer and partner authentication workflows
    Provide real-time verification responses

    Track every product across the supply chain.

    Traceability links lifecycle events into a continuous product history. Organizations can trace a product’s journey from origin to customer interaction, improving supply chain visibility and supporting compliance requirements.

    Track product movement across supply chain participants
    Maintain end-to-end traceability records
    Support regulatory and compliance reporting
    Enable investigation of anomalies and supply chain issues

    Generate unique identities for every product unit.

    Serialization assigns a unique identifier to each product unit, creating the foundation for authentication, lifecycle tracking, and traceability. Zareeva generates serialized identifiers that can be embedded in QR codes, barcodes, or digital labels attached to products.

    QR codes and barcode identity formats
    High-volume identity issuance

    Persistent identity layer
    Secure identity references
    Batch and range-based identity allocation
    Support for GS1 serialization standards
    Collision-safe serial number generation
    Distributed identity issuance across manufacturing sites
